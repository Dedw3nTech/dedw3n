Dedw3n Cookie and Tracker Overview
Essential/Necessary Cookies
These cookies are always active and cannot be disabled:

dedwen_cookie_consent - Stores user's cookie consent preferences
dedwen_first_visit - Tracks if user is a first-time visitor
Session cookies - For user authentication and login state
CSRF tokens - Security cookies for form submissions
Analytics Cookies (Optional - User Consent Required)
Google Analytics cookies (when enabled):
_ga, _gid, _gat - Standard Google Analytics tracking
These are disabled when analytics: false in consent
Marketing/Advertising Cookies (Optional - User Consent Required)
_fbp, _fbc - Facebook Pixel tracking cookies
_hjid, _hjFirstSeen - Hotjar user session tracking
IDE, test_cookie - Google advertising cookies
Targeting cookies - For personalized ads and content
Performance Cookies (Optional)
Performance tracking cookies - Monitor site performance and user interactions
Cache optimization cookies - Improve page load times
Functional Cookies (Optional)
Language preference cookies - Store user's language selection
UI preference cookies - Remember user interface settings
Social media cookies - Enable content sharing with Facebook, Instagram
Cookie Consent Management System
The platform implements a comprehensive GDPR/CCPA compliant cookie management system:

Global Privacy Control (GPC) Support - Automatically respects browser privacy signals
Granular Control - Users can accept/reject specific cookie categories
California Privacy Rights - Special provisions for CCPA compliance
Automatic Cookie Clearing - Removes tracking cookies when consent is withdrawn
Third-Party Services That May Set Cookies
Google Services - Analytics, advertising, fonts
Facebook/Meta - Social sharing, advertising pixels
Hotjar - User experience analytics
CDN providers - Content delivery optimization
Data Collection Purposes
The cookies facilitate collecting:

User behavior and interaction patterns
Device and browser information
Geolocation data (with consent)
Marketing attribution data
Performance metrics
Security and fraud prevention data
User Control Options
Users can:

Accept all cookies
Accept only necessary cookies
Customize preferences by category
Withdraw consent at any time
View detailed information about each cookie type
The platform provides transparent cookie management with clear opt-out mechanisms and respects user privacy choices through its advanced consent management system.
